This Shiny App was developed to create Age-dependent Reference Intervals using different methods (LMS, GAMLSS, Window-Methods and Regression). By GAMLSS-Models the distribution of the data can be freely selected from: Box-Cox Cole and Green Distribution "BCCG", Box-Cox Power Exponential Distribution "BCPE" or Box-Cox t Distribution "BCT" and additionally Normal-Distribution, Log-Normal-Distribution. The models are created with the package gamlss. The GAMLSS models have different smooth additive terms (P-Splines, Cubic Splines, Polynomials (Degree 3 and 4), Neural Network and Decision Tree). Currently, three Window-Methods are available regular windows, windows connected to a Decision Tree and the Sliding Window-method. And four different Regressions (Linear, Polynomials (Degree 3,4 and 10)). Different metrics can be used to compare the models:

- Akaike Information Criterion (AIC)
- Bayesian Information Criterion (BIC) / Schwatz Bayesian Criterion (SBC)
- Generalized Infromation Criterion (GAIC) with k=3
- Pseudo R-Squared (R2)
- R-Squared (R2)
- Mean Absolute Error
- Mean Squared Error
- Root mean squared error

All data can be downloaded in CSV format, when it is open in a Browser, also all figures as PNG or selected as EPS/JPEG (by Plotly use the menu in the right corner) or the page as PDF with a right click. At each tab is an information about the method or go to the Wiki .
